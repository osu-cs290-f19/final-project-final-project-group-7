Interview: Albert Dupontel

Dave Strauss     26/8/2019

Albert Dupontel, director of "See you up there" said that Berlin Film Fest is one of the best that he saw, and that Germany is country that he will gladly return to, primarily because of people he met.
"Beeing in Germany is something that I will remember till the end of my life, I'm grateful to crew of BFF for inviting me and I*m glad that my movie will be shown for German audience" he said.
