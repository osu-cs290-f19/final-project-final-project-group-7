BLACK BUTTERFLY

 Thriler | 93 min

Start preview : 10.05.2019

Short summary: Outside a mountain town grappling with a series of abductions and murders, Paul, a reclusive writer, struggles to start what he hopes will be a career-saving screenplay.

Actors: Antonio Banderas, Jonathan Rhys Meyers, Piper Perabo

Director: Brian Goodman


FANTASTIC WOMAN

 Drama | 100 min

Start preview : 24.05.2019

Short summary: Marina is a young waitress and wannabe singer. Orlando owns a printing company. Together they plan their future. When Orlando dies suddenly, Marina has to stand up to his family and society to show them what she truly is: a complex, strong, forthright and... fantastic woman.

Actors: Daniela Vega, Francisco Reyes, Luis Gnecco, Aline Küppenheim

Director: Sebastián Lelio


SEE YOU UP THERE

 Drama | 117 min

Start preview : 24.05.2019

Short summary: November 1919. Two survivors of the trenches set up a scam based on war memorials. One is a brilliant illustrator, the other an unassuming accountant. In the France of the Roaring Twenties, their operation becomes as dangerous as it is spectacular.

Actors: Nahuel Pérez Biscayart, Albert Dupontel, Laurent Lafitte

Director: Albert Dupontel


THE NILE HILTON INCIDENT

 Thriler | 106 min

Start preview : 28.02.2019

Short summary: Cairo 2011, just weeks before the revolution. Police Detective Noredin, working in the in-famous Kasr el-Nil Police Station, is handed the case of a murdered singer. He soon realizes that the investigation concerns the very power elite, close to the President’s inner circle.

Actors: Fares Fares, Mari Malek, Yasser Ali Maher

Director:Tarik Saleh
