See you up there

Original name: Au revoir là-haut
Start showing: 24.05.2019
Duration: 117 min
Country: FRA
Genre: Drama
Actors: Nahuel Pérez Biscayart, Albert Dupontel, Laurent Lafitte
Director: Albert Dupontel

Summary

November 1919. Two survivors of the trenches set up a scam based on war memorials. One is a brilliant illustrator, the other an unassuming accountant. In the France of the Roaring Twenties, their operation becomes as dangerous as it is spectacular. Albert Dupontel is a French actor and director who began his career as a monologue artist. His first film, Bernie earned him a place as a new voice in French cinema. He continued to alternate his eclectic career as an actor with directing his own films.
