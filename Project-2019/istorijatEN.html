Buch Kesidi were the last preformers on 9. BFF


Young bend from Berlin had the honor to play their music on closing night of last years Berlin Film Fest. In German capital Berlin, in front of cuople thousend people, they played songs from their album "Posesivno-ospulsivni hospul". Hits like "Jaa (imam plaan)", "Draga" i "Kafe aparat" were echoing in Jungen Hause in Berlin in celebration of ending festival. "We are glad that we had the honor to be the last one to play at BFF, atmosphere was magnificant" - said frontman Luka Reich.

Award winners declared

As the competition part of BFF is done, the last day is saved for declaring winners. Jury which consist of Simona Nobile, Jelena Angelovski, Vladan Petkov and Marc Grba has watched all the movies and made their decision which actors and directors will get awarded. Rewards are, as always, split in two categories, "Lighthouse" for feature-length movies (only for best movie) and "Early works" for short movies in which there are more categories.



Award Lighthouse:
  • Best movie: Popstar (John Nesbit)

Award Early works:
  • Best movie: The room (Peter Smith)
  • Best director: Mike Zarubica (7 days in hell)
  • Best screenplaz: Dave Dans (The turnament)
  • Best actress: Johana Strauss (New girl)
